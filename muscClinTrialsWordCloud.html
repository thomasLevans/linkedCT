Word Cloud of Keywords for MUSC Clinical Trials on LinkedCT.gov
    The Breakdown

    This is the initial implementation of a word cloud driven visualization. The data is courtesy of LinkedCT.gov. By querying the SQARQL endpoint, all data pertinent to clinical trials participated in by the Medical University of South Carolina has been compiled. By examining the predicates surrounding a trial entity it seemed useful to break partition the MUSC trials by year. Furthermore each year displays all keywords associated with all trials for the given year, allowing the user to view the year's body of trials from a topical perspective. By clicking on a keyword a transition will trigger displaying the specific trial the keyword is linked to, and all other associated keywords for the trial. Mousing over the trial will display a detailed tooltip. Double clicking either the trial node, or the keywords will transition the visualization back to it's previous state.
